Board Meetings

The board currently meets Tuesday evenings via Google Hangouts.

Annual General Meeting

The date for the Annual General Meeting (AGM) has not yet been set. It is required to occur between 23rd April 2014 and 23rd April 2015.

Board Elections

Around each AGM a new board is voted in by the Association's membership. Any member may self-nominate for a board position. No hard and fast details have been set regarding the election. It is likely, however, that there will be a nomination period of about a month, followed by a voting period of a few weeks, immediately prior to the AGM.
