Max Kaye

Treasurer

As a cryptocurrency enthusiast, and a member of the Bitcoin community since 2010, Max has a vast array of experience dealing with *coin based technology; from running a NMC/BTC mining pool in 2011, to experimenting with trustless exchange in 2013.
Through the Bitcoin Association, he hopes to foster the natural social structures that could form around an economy based on Bitcoin.

Adam Poulton

Secretary

Adam is a self employed business owner and father of two living in Tasmania. Adam has a Degree in Applied Science from the University of Tasmania and has also had an interest in money and macro economics from an early age.
Since the global financial crisis in 2008, Adam has been studying economics, in particular the theories of the Austrian School. This has lead to his interest in Bitcoin, and his belief that this new currency can solve many of the worlds current economic problems. Adam is currently in Beta in a Bitcoin startup, designed to integrate Bitcoin into peoples daily lives, and hopes that in the future, his children will be more familiar with Bitcoin than with regular dollars.

Pantelis Roussakis

Vice President

Pantelis Roussakis is an Australian web and digital marketing specialist, social media speaker and corporate trainer. I have been a Bitcoin enthusiast for 3 years.
Crypto currencies have the potential to change the way we transact. I believe that Bitcoin and crypto-currencies per say represent a practical way wealth can created and controlled by individuals. The wide acceptance of Bitcoin in Australia will come about when Government and Business groups , can understand and discuss their needs within the new Bitcoin economy. Bitcoin Australia is that place.
I bring my visual communication and marketing skills to Bitcoin Australia so we can create general awareness, educate and update our members and the wider community.

Jason Williams

President

Jason has been passionate about IT from a young age and has been involved with emerging technologies from early stages including the roll out of cable internet in Australia.
He believes Bitcoin is a technology set to revolutionise the world and is ardent in his desire for it to grow and mature into an everyday technology for everybody. With a career spanning 20 years, he brings experience from the banking and ISP sectors (both in Australia and from Europe) to the Bitcoin Association of Australia

Tristan Winters

Board Member

Graduated law school politically aware but largely apathetic. After discovering ‘The Money Masters’, by William T. Still, he was awakened and energised. He tirelessly consumed the writings of free-market thinkers. His worldview solidified. Only honest money could beget freedom.
Bitcoin is honest money. Bitcoin is outside the curb of state. With nurture, Bitcoin could make us free. Since discovering Bitcoin he has dedicated himself to that nurture, manifesting the dream of free men. Tristan is actively involved in the Bitcoin community in Australia and Europe, across several projects.
